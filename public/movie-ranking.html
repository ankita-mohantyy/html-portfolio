ANKITA'S FAVOURITE MOVIES

My top 3 movies of all time

Taare Zameen Par

This movie is a masterpiece as it put stress on the rat race of kids nowadays.It beautifully explains that each person is built differently and should not be compared. It is definitely a must watch

Sita Ramam

First of all it is a romantic movie and the cherry on the top is it being based on a soldier's life. Even the casting and story telling is just awesome.

Hanu-Man

Hanu-Man basically has a blend of comedy and emotions.This movie will be proved very interesting for the mythology lovers.Its cinematography is a perfect treat to the eyes
